About me

Like all people from Tenerife, I love the sea, the good weather and the peace. My passion for computer science starts since I was a kid, when I wondered how it was possible that a simple box could do such incredible things as simulate a whole and complex world, communicate with someone on the other side of the world instantly, etc.

To solve all those questions, and to be able to transform all of the above from "magic" to "normal", I decided to start my studies in computer science. My experience in the degree is generally satisfactory and I have managed to open that mysterious black box to understand everything that makes up a software.

In the last years of my degree, I specialized in software engineering. This has allowed me to abstract from the concrete process of software programming to understand and structure problems at a higher level. It has also allowed me to know the most useful and demanded project management tools in the market.

Currently, I work as a Java developer in a project with IBM and Banco Santander and I keep training in online education platforms to be always at the forefront of the latest technologies and methodologies that come to the market.
